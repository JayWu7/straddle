Massive Gap: Crypto Options vs. Traditional Financial Options Trading Volume

In traditional finance, options trading represents a substantial share of the derivatives market, whereas in the crypto space, options trading constitutes only about 1/130th of the trading volume compared to perpetual contracts. (According to data from Binance and Deribit).

This indicates substantial growth prospects for cryptocurrency options trading in the future. With the potential approval of a BTC ETF next year and the involvement of traditional giants like BlackRock, crypto options trading volumes are set to skyrocket.
